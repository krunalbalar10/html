Story 2

Once, in the sacred forests of Kailash, Lord Shiva, known as Shankar Bhagwan, embarked on a profound meditation. His deep trance summoned a majestic lion, Garjan, who approached with reverence.

Garjan, born of Shiva's divine energy, sought wisdom. Shankar Bhagwan, pleased by the lion's devotion, imparted profound teachings. Garjan learned the art of strength combined with compassion, symbolizing Shiva's attributes.

Years passed, and Garjan roamed the world, protecting the weak and defending righteousness. He became a symbol of courage and benevolence. Shankar Bhagwan smiled, for Garjan embodied the harmonious balance of strength and compassion, a reflection of the divine himself.
